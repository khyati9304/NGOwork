About Us

Basti Ki Pathshala is a grassroots educational initiative aimed at providing free and quality education to children from underserved communities. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty.

Our dedicated team of volunteers conducts daily classes, distributes educational materials, and organizes awareness drives to uplift and empower children in urban bastis.
